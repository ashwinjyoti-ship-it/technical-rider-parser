📊 WHAT IT DOES:

INPUT:  Technical Rider (PDF/DOCX/TXT)
   ↓
PARSE:  Extract equipment with brand detection
   ↓
COUNT:  Aggregate quantities by brand
   ↓
DISPLAY: Show organized breakdown
   ↓
EXPORT: Generate professional Word report
